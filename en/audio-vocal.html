Frequently Asked Questions

How good is the vocal extraction quality?

This tool uses Phase Cancellation, so quality may be lower than AI-based tools (e.g., Demucs). It works best when vocals are clearly panned to the center in a stereo track. Center-panned instruments may also appear in the output.

Does my audio file get uploaded?

No. All processing happens entirely within your browser — your files are never uploaded to any server. This zero-server architecture keeps your data 100% private, safe for sensitive documents and enterprise security environments.
