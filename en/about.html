About WooaAudio

Free online audio toolkit — convert, cut, merge, record and more, all in your browser

🎵 What is WooaAudio?

WooaAudio is a free online audio tool service that makes it easy to work with audio files directly in your web browser — no app installation or account needed.

From format conversion and audio editing to recording, waveform visualization, ringtone creation, and instrument tuning — WooaAudio covers a wide range of audio tasks. All processing happens locally in your browser, so your files are never sent to any server.

🎧 Available Tools

🔄 Format Convert ✂️ Audio Cutter 🔗 Audio Merger 🔊 Volume Control ⚡ Speed Control 🌅 Fade In/Out 🎼 Pitch Changer 🎙️ Audio Recorder 📳 Ringtone Maker 📊 Waveform Viewer ℹ️ Audio Info 🏷️ Tag Editor 🎤 MR Maker 🥁 Metronome 🎸 Instrument Tuner 🗣️ Text to Speech 📊 Decibel Meter + More ›

⚡ Key Features

  • 🔒 Complete Privacy — Audio files never leave your browser. All processing is done locally — 100% private.
  • 🚀 Fast Processing — No server upload wait. Files are processed locally at full speed regardless of your internet connection.
  • 💸 Completely Free — All features are free with no limits. No account, no login, no payment required.
  • 📱 Works on Any Device — Fully responsive design for PC, laptop, tablet, and smartphone.
  • 🌐 No Installation — Just open your browser and go. Nothing to download or install.

🛠️ Technology

WooaAudio is built with modern web technologies. Audio conversion and editing is powered by FFmpeg.wasm, while recording, waveform visualization, metronome, and tuner features use the Web Audio API. All processing runs client-side — no files are ever sent to a server.

Supported browsers: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (latest versions recommended)
